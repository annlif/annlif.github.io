Gennaro Parlato








Short Bio

 Gennaro Parlato is an Associate Professor of Computer Science at the
University of Molise (UNIMOL), in the Department of Biosciences and
Territory (DiBT), and the director of the Program Analysis in Clouds Lab
(PAC Lab). Before joining UNIMOL, Gennaro enjoyed a
long international research career.  Between 2006 and 2011,
Gennaro worked as a postdoc research associate, first in the
Department of Computer Science at UIUC (Urbana, USA,
May 2006-March 2010), and then in the Modeling and Verification
research group at LIAFA and now IRIF Laboratory (Paris, France,
April 2010-April 2011). Beteween 2011 and the end of 2018,
Gennaro was an academic of the University of Southampton (SOTON)
in the Department of Electronics and Computer Science (ECS), first as a
Lecturer and then as an Associate Professor. While at the
University of Southampton, Gennaro was also a member of the
GCHQ/EPSRC Academic Centre of Excellence for
Cybersecurity Research. Gennaro received his doctorate in
Computer Science from the University of Salerno, Italy, in April 2006.

Gennaro Parlato








Short Bio

Gennaro Parlato is an Associate Professor of Computer Science at the
University of Molise (UNIMOL), in the Department of Biosciences and
Territory (DiBT), and the director of the Program Analysis in Clouds Lab
(PAC Lab). Before joining UNIMOL, Gennaro enjoyed a
long international research career.  Between 2006 and 2011,
Gennaro worked as a postdoc research associate, first in the
Department of Computer Science at UIUC (Urbana, USA,
May 2006-March 2010), and then in the Modeling and Verification
research group at LIAFA and now IRIF Laboratory (Paris, France,
April 2010-April 2011). Beteween 2011 and the end of 2018,
Gennaro was an academic of the University of Southampton (SOTON)
in the Department of Electronics and Computer Science (ECS), first as a
Lecturer and then as an Associate Professor. While at the
University of Southampton, Gennaro was also a member of the
GCHQ/EPSRC Academic Centre of Excellence for
Cybersecurity Research. Gennaro received his doctorate in
Computer Science from the University of Salerno, Italy, in April 2006.

Short Bio

{Anna Lisa Ferrara} holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science, from the University of Salerno, Italy. Since 2020, she is an Associate Professor with the Department of Biosciences, Division of Computer Science, University of Molise, Italy. Before joining the University of Molise, she has been a Lecturer in Cyber Security at the University of Southampton, UK and previously at the University of Surrey, UK. She has been a member of both Surrey and Southampton Centres of Excellence in Cyber Security Research recognised by the UK Government Communications Headquarters (GCHQ). Anna Lisa has been a member of the Southampton Cyber Security Academy and a member of the EPSRC CryptoForma network of Excellence. Before joining the University of Surrey, she has been a Research Assistant within the Crypto Group at the University of Bristol (Nov 2011 - Sep 2014) and a Post-Doctorate Fellow at the following institutions: School of Electronics and Computer Science, University of Southampton, UK, (May 2011 - Oct 2011); Department of Computer Science, University of Salerno, Italy, (Jun 2009 - May 2011); Department of Computer Science, University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign, USA, (Feb 2008 - April 2009); Security and Privacy Applied Research Group, Johns Hopkins University, USA (Feb 2007 - Jan 2008). Anna Lisa is the recipient of a EPSRC New Investigator Award and recipient of a Microsoft Azure Research Award. Anna Lisa is a member of the IFIP WG 11.3 Working Group on Data and Applications Security and Privacy. She has served as program committee member of various international conferences.
